HOW TO PLAY...

CHN LNK is a spin-off on the popular Chain Link game but with a twist.

Every word on the board forms a chained link with the word below it. The objective of the game is to identify all the words in the chain. If the letter you selected is part of the CHAIN, the corresponding tiles will be highlighted in Green.

However, here come the Twist! VOWELS are disabled till you figure out all the OTHER LETTERS in the chain.
