HOW TO PLAY...

CHN LNK is a spin-off on the popular Chain Link game, but with a Twist.
  • The objective of the game is to identify all the words in the CHAIN. Every word on the board LINKS logically, with the word below it.

  • If the letter you selected is in ANY WORD of the chain, the corresponding tiles will be highlighted in Green.

  • However, here come the Twist! VOWELS are disabled till you figure out all the OTHER LETTERS in the chain. The tiles on the grid that ultimately hold a vowel are highlighted in Red.

  • Each incorrect letter will cost you a Life. You have a total of 5 lives that is depicted using üî¥ on the Game Board.
